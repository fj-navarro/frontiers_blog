Frontiers in Business Transformation.

FiBT aims to become a participatory research project that accommodates the visions and contributions of relevant actors in the field of business transformation from academia, the executive management of organizations, and the IT and consulting industry. It is expected that the contributions obtained through collaborative work will allow the results to be published regularly in videos, posts, and reports.

This initiative is independent and beyond the interests of any third party, and does not have resources of any kind from the entities mentioned on the site, beyond those provided by the author himself.
